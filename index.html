Sounds of Empathy

One of the main characteristics which defines us as human beings is our ability for social-bonding and working together to achieve common goals. The AVPR1a gene has been shown to play a role in this behaviour. Nicknamed the “altruism gene,” it has also been identified in other species that exhibit strong social bonds. A variant of this gene seems to lead to ruthless behaviour.
